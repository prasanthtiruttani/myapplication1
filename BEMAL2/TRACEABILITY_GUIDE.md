## 📋 WHAT IS TRACEABILITY?

Traceability means tracking **WHO** used **WHAT** consumable, **WHEN**, **HOW MUCH**, and **FOR WHAT PURPOSE**.

This creates a complete audit trail of all consumable usage in your laboratory.

---

## ✨ KEY FEATURES

### 1. **Usage Recording**
Track every time a consumable is used:

## 🎯 HOW TO USE USAGE TRACKING

### Step 1: Access Usage Tracking
1. Login to CTS
2. Go to **"Usage Tracking"** in navigation menu
3. Click **"+ Record Usage"** button

### Step 2: Record a Usage
Fill in the form:

**Field** | **Description** | **Example**
----------|-----------------|------------
**Consumable Used** | Select which item was used | Printer Paper A4
**Quantity Used** | How much was consumed | 5 (reams)
**Department** | Which department used it | Research & Development
**Purpose** | Why was it used | Printing test reports
**Notes** | Additional details (optional) | For battery testing documentation

### Step 3: Submit
- Click **"Record Usage"**
- System automatically updates stock level
- Record is added to history

## 📊 VIEWING USAGE DATA

### 1. **Consumption History Table**
Shows all usage records:
- Date & Time of usage
- Which consumable was used
- Quantity consumed
- Who used it
- Purpose of usage
- Department
- Remaining stock after usage

### 2. **User-wise Consumption Summary**
Shows each user's total consumption:
- **User Name** - Who used consumables
- **Department** - Their department
- **Total Items Used** - Quantity they consumed
- **Usage Count** - How many times they used items
- **Last Used** - Last date they used something
- **View Details** - Click to see all their usage

### 3. **Consumable-wise Usage Analysis**
Shows each consumable's usage pattern:
- **Consumable Name** - Which item
- **Total Used** - Total quantity consumed
- **Usage Frequency** - How many times it was used
- **Last Used By** - Who used it last
- **Last Used Date** - When it was last used
- **View History** - Click to see complete history
