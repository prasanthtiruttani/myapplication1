About CTS

Consumable Tracking System

CTS is an efficient consumable management platform designed for Amara Raja Energy & Mobility Ltd to track office and operational consumables across all departments. It streamlines procurement, monitors stock levels, and reduces wastage through intelligent tracking and analytics.

Key Benefits

  • ✅ Real-time inventory tracking across all departments
  • ✅ Automated low stock alerts and reorder notifications
  • ✅ Centralized supplier management and procurement
  • ✅ Request and approval workflow automation
  • ✅ Cost optimization through consumption analytics
  • ✅ Secure access with role-based permissions
